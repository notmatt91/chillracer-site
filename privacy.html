Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Overview

Chill Racer is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

  • Account information: Username, email address, and encrypted password when you create an account.
  • Game progress: Race results, car collection, campaign progress, and tournament participation stored on our servers.
  • Purchase records: Transaction IDs from Apple App Store for in-app purchases. We do not store payment card details.
  • Device information: Basic device type and OS version for compatibility and debugging purposes.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Health or fitness data
  • Browsing history
  • Advertising identifiers (we do not run targeted ads)

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain your game account
  • To process in-app purchases and deliver content
  • To operate tournaments and leaderboards
  • To improve game performance and fix bugs

Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store: Handles payment processing for in-app purchases.
  • Google AdMob: Serves optional rewarded ads (watch ads for in-game gas). AdMob may collect device identifiers as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

Your game data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can request account deletion at any time by contacting support.

Children's Privacy

Chill Racer does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at chillracergame@gmail.com.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, reach us at chillracergame@gmail.com.
